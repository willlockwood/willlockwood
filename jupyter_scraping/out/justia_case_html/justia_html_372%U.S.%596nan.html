TAR ASPHALT TRUCKING CO., INC., v. UNITED STATES ET AL.

NEW JERSEY. No. 762.
Decided March 25, 1963.

208 F. Supp. 611, affirmed.

John J. Corcoran, Jr. for appellant.

PETERSON v. ALLEN CIRCUIT COURT ET AL.
ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE SUPREME COURT OF INDIANA.
No. 415, Misc.
Decided March 25, 1963.

Certiorari granted; judgment vacated; and case remanded.
