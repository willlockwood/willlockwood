OREGON EX REL. SMITH v. GLADDEN, 368 U.S. 8 (1961)

368 U.S. 8

OREGON EX REL. SMITH v. GLADDEN, WARDEN.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF OREGON.
No. 23, Misc.
Decided October 9, 1961.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Appellant pro se.

Robert Y. Thornton, Attorney General of Oregon, and Harold W. Adams, Assistant Attorney General, for appellee.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
