HOLMES v. SUPERIOR COURT OF CALIFORNIA, CITY AND COUNTY OF SAN FRANCISCO.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF CALIFORNIA.
No. 1063, Misc.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

PER CURIAM.
