U.S. Supreme Court

OLEN v. OLEN, 370 U.S. 721 (1962)

370 U.S. 721

OLEN v. OLEN.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF NEW JERSEY.
No. 1153, Misc.
Decided June 25, 1962.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

PER CURIAM.

The appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.

MR. JUSTICE FRANKFURTER took no part in the consideration or decision of this case.

U.S. Supreme Court

WINTERS v. OHIO, 370 U.S. 721 (1962)

370 U.S. 721

WINTERS v. OHIO.
ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE SUPREME COURT OF OHIO.
No. 388, Misc.
Decided June 25, 1962.

Certiorari granted; judgment vacated; case remanded for further proceedings.

Petitioner pro se.

Richard O. Harris for respondent.

PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the Supreme Court of Ohio for further proceedings. Griffin v. Illinois, 351 U.S. 12.

MR. JUSTICE FRANKFURTER took no part in the consideration or decision of this case.
