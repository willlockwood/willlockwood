IN RE RETENELLER, 355 U.S. 183 (1957)

355 U.S. 183

IN RE RETENELLER.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF PENNSYLVANIA, EASTERN DISTRICT.
No. 546.
Decided December 9, 1957.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Jacob J. Kilimnik for appellant.

David Berger filed a motion for the City of Philadelphia to dismiss the appeal.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
