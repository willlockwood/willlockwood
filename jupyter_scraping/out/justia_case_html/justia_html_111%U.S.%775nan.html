GREENHOOD v. RANDALL, 111 U.S. 775 (1884)

111 U.S. 775

GREENHOOD and others
v.
RANDALL.

May 5, 1884

Edwin W. Toole, for plaintiff in error.

S. Shellabarger and J. M. Wilson, for defendant in error.

WAITE, C. J.
