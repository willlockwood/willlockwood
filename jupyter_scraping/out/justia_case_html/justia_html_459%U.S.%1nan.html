DECREE\n

\n

For the purpose of giving effect to the decision of this Court herein, announced June 18, 1982, 457 U. S. 457 U.S. 273, rehearing having been denied on September 9, 1982, 458 U.S. 1131,

\n

IT IS ORDERED, ADJUDGED, AND DECREED:

\n

1. As against the State of California and all those claiming under it, the United State holds all right, title and interest in the parcel of land described in the Complaint filed herein and in Exhibit A hereto, the seaward boundary of which shall be the line of mean high water of the Pacific Ocean, as heretofore or hereafter modified by accretion, erosion or reliction, whether attributable to natural or artificial causes;

\n

2. The State of California has no right, title or interest in the said parcel of land, and the State, its agencies, political

Page 459 U. S. 2

subdivisions, officers and agents, and all those claiming under them or in privity with them, are enjoined from interfering in any way with the right, title, and interest of the United States in the said parcel;

\n

3. Each party shall bear its own costs.

\n
