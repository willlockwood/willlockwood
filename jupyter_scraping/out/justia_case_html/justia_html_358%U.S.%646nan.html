358 U.S. 646

RISER v. WARDEN, CALIFORNIA STATE PRISON.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF CALIFORNIA.
No. 331, Misc.
Decided February 24, 1959.

Appeal dismissed for want of a substantial federal question.

Reported below: See 47 Cal. 2d 566.

George T. Davis for appellant.

Stanley Mosk, Attorney General of California, Clarence A. Linn, Chief Assistant Attorney General, and Arlo E. Smith, Deputy Attorney General, for appellee.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed for want of a substantial federal question.
