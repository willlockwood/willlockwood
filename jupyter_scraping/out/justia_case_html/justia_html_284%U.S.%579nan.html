BRANNAN v. HARRISON, 284 U.S. 579 (1931)

284 U.S. 579

L. S. BRANNAN, M. N. Carroll, D. O. Hamby, et al., appellants,
v.
William B. HARRISON, as Comptroller General, etc.
No. 169.

Supreme Court of the United States

October 26, 1931

Mr. C. N. Davie, of Atlanta, Ga., for appellants.

PER CURIAM.

The appeal herein is dismissed. Louisiana Navigation Co. Ltd., v. Oyster Commission, 226 U.S. 99, 101, 33 S. Ct. 78; Reddall v. Bryan, 24 How. 420; Bruce v. Tobin, 245 U.S. 18, 19, 38 S. Ct. 7; Verden v. Coleman, 18 How. 86; Moses v. The Mayor, 15 Wall. 387.
