U.S. Supreme Court

CALLENDER v. FLORIDA, 380 U.S. 519 (1965)

380 U.S. 519

CALLENDER ET AL. v. FLORIDA.
ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE SUPREME COURT OF FLORIDA.
No. 58.
Decided April 26, 1965.

Certiorari granted and judgments reversed.

Carl Rachlin and Floyd McKissick for petitioners.

James W. Kynes, Attorney General of Florida, and George R. Georgieff, Assistant Attorney General, for respondent.

PER CURIAM.

The petition for writ of certiorari is granted and the judgments are reversed. Boynton v. Virginia, 364 U.S. 454, Abernathy v. Alabama, ante, p. 447.
