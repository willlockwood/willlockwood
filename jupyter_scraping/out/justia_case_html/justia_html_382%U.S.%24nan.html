THOMPSON v. CITY AND STATE OF NEW YORK, 382 U.S. 24 (1965)

382 U.S. 24

THOMPSON v. CITY AND STATE OF NEW YORK.
APPEAL FROM THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE SECOND CIRCUIT.
No. 248, Misc.
Decided October 11, 1965.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

PER CURIAM.

The appeal is dismissed for want of jurisdiction. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
