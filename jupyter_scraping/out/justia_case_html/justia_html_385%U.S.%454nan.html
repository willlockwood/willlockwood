FEDERATED DEPARTMENT STORES, INC. v. GEROSA, COMPTROLLER OF THE
CITY OF NEW YORK.
APPEAL FROM THE COURT OF APPEALS OF NEW YORK. No. 171.
Decided January 9, 1967.

16 N. Y. 2d 320, 213 N. E. 2d 677; 17 N. Y. 2d 579, 215 N. E. 2d 516, appeal dismissed.

Wilbur H. Friedman and George G. Gallantz for appellant.
