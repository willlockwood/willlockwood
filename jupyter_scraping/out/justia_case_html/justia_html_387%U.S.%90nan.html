U.S. Supreme Court

RUBIO v. UNITED STATES, 387 U.S. 90 (1967)

387 U.S. 90

RUBIO v. UNITED STATES.
ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR
THE SEVENTH CIRCUIT. No. 710, Misc.
Decided May 15, 1967.

Certiorari granted; judgment reversed.

Petitioner pro se.

Solicitor General Marshall for the United States.

PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is reversed. Coppedge v. United States, 369 U.S. 438.
