U.S. Supreme Court

MISSOURI, K & T R CO v. EVANS, 175 U.S. 723 (1899)

175 U.S. 723

MISSOURI, KANSAS & TEXAS RAILWAY COMPANY OF TEXAS, Plaintiff in Error,
v.
J. M. EVANS.
No. 78.

November 6, 1899

Messrs. James Hagerman and J. M. Bryson for plaintiff in error.

Messrs. Rush Taggart and H. Chilton for defendant in error.

Dismissed with costs, on the authority of Mason v. United States, 136 U.S. 581, 34 L. ed. 545, 10 Sup. Ct. Rep. 1062; Hardee v. Wilson, 146 U.S. 179, 36 L. ed. 933, 13 Sup. Ct. Rep. 39; and Sipperley v. Smith, 155 U.S. 86, 39 L. ed. 79, 15 Sup. Ct. Rep. 15.1
