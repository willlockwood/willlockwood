CERTIORARI TO THE SUPREME COURT OF COLORADO\n

\n

Certiorari dismissed for want of jurisdiction. Reported below: 633 P.2d 455.

\n

PER CURIAM.

\n

Under 28 U.S.C. \xc2\xa7 1257, this Court has jurisdiction to review only "[f]inal judgments or decrees rendered by the highest court of a State in which a decision could be had." Because the Colorado Supreme Court remanded this case for trial, its decision is not final "as an effective determination of the litigation." Market Street R. Co. v. Railroad Comm\'n of Cal., 324 U. S. 548, 324 U. S. 551 (1945). Although there is a limited set of situations in which we have found finality as to the federal issue despite the ordering of further proceedings in the lower state courts, see Cox Broadcasting Corp. v. Cohn, 420 U. S. 469 (1975), this case does not fit into any of those categories. We therefore dismiss for want of jurisdiction.

\n

It is so ordered.
