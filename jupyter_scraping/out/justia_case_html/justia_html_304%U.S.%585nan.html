v.
IGLEHEART BROTHERS, Incorporated.*
No. 1015.

Supreme Court of the United States

May 31, 1938

Messrs. Emra H. Ireland, of Evansville, Ind., and Albert Stump, of Indianapolis, Ind., for petitioner.
