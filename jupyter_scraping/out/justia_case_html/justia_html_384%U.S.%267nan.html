384 U.S. 267

RUTHERFORD v. WASHINGTON.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF WASHINGTON.
No. 1167.
Decided May 23, 1966.

66 Wash. 2d 851, 405 P.2d 719, appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Kenneth A. MacDonald for appellant.

PER CURIAM.
