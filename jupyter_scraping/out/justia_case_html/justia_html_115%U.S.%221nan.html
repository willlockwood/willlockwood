\n

MR. CHIEF JUSTICE WAITE delivered the opinion of the Court.

\n

When this cause was reached on the call of the docket, it was submitted by the defendant in error on a printed brief. An assignment of errors was not annexed to or returned with the writ of error, as required by \xc2\xa7 997, Rev.Stat. At the last term, the counsel for the plaintiff in error was permitted to withdraw his appearance, and no one has taken his place. No argument has been submitted in behalf of the plaintiff in error, and no errors have been assigned in any form. We therefore affirm the judgment without opening the record.

\n

Affirmed.
