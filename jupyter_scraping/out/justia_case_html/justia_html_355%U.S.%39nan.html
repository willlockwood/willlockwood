U.S. Supreme Court

DISTRICT LODGE 34 v. CAVETT CO., 355 U.S. 39 (1957)

355 U.S. 39

DISTRICT LODGE 34, LODGE 804, INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF MACHINISTS,
ET AL. v. L. P. CAVETT CO.
ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE SUPREME COURT OF OHIO. No. 453.
Decided November 12, 1957.

Certiorari granted and judgment reversed on authority of cases cited.

Reported below: 166 Ohio St. 508, 143 N. E. 2d 840.

J. W. Brown and Plato E. Papps for petitioners.

Leonard A. Weakley for respondent.

PER CURIAM.

The petition for writ of certiorari is granted and the judgment of the Supreme Court of Ohio is reversed. Amalgamated Meat Cutters v. Fairlawn Meats, Inc., 353 U.S. 20, 948; Weber v. Anheuser-Busch, Inc., 348 U.S. 468; Garner v. Teamsters Union, 346 U.S. 485.
