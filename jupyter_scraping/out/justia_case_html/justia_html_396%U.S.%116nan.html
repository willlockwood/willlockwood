No. 617.
Decided December 8, 1969

___ W. Va. ___, 167 S. E. 2d 890, appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

C. E. Goodwin for appellant.

Chauncey H. Browning, Jr., Attorney General of West Virginia, and William F. Carroll, Assistant Attorney General, for appellee.
