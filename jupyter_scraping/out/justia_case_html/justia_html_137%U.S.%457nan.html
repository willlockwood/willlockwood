U.S. Supreme Court

PENNSYLVANIA CO, EX PARTE, 137 U.S. 457 (1890)

137 U.S. 457

Ex parte PENNSYLVANIA CO.

December 8, 1890.

Page 137 U.S. 457, 458

Danl. Davenport and William H. O\'Hara, for petitioner.

Lewis E. Stanton, for respondent.

BRADLEY, J.

This case in all material respects is identical with the case of Ex parte Pennsylvania Co., ante, 141, (just decided,) and the same conclusion is reached as in that case. The petition for mandamus is denied.
