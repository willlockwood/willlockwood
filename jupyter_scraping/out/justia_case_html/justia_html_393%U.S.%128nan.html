U.S. Supreme Court

CROSS v. COURT OF APPEAL OF CALIFORNIA, 393 U.S. 128 (1968)

393 U.S. 128

CROSS ET AL. v.
COURT OF APPEAL OF CALIFORNIA, FIRST APPELLATE DISTRICT, ET AL.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF CALIFORNIA. No. 533, Misc.
Decided November 12, 1968.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

PER CURIAM.

The appeal is dismissed for want of jurisdiction. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for a writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
