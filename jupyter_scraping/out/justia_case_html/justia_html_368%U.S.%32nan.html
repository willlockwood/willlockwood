SCHROEDER v. WILLIAMS, 368 U.S. 32 (1961)

368 U.S. 32

SCHROEDER ET AL. v. WILLIAMS ET AL.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF NEBRASKA.
No. 357.
Decided November 6, 1961.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Reported below: 171 Neb. 792, 107 N. W. 2d 750.

James A. Lake, Sr. and Roy Harrop for appellants.

Charles Thone and Clark O\'Hanlon for appellees.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
