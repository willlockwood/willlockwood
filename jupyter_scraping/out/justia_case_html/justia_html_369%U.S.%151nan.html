U.S. Supreme Court

HARDING v. HAND, 369 U.S. 151 (1962)

369 U.S. 151

HARDING v. HAND, WARDEN.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF KANSAS.
No. 5, Misc.
Decided March 19, 1962.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Appellant pro se.

John Anderson, Jr., Attorney General of Kansas, and J. Richard Foth, Assistant Attorney General, for appellee.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.

MR. JUSTICE WHITTAKER took no part in the consideration or decision of this case.
