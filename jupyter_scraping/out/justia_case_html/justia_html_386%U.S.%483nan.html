CONNOR ET AL. v. JOHNSON, GOVERNOR OF MISSISSIPPI, ET AL.

265 F. Supp. 492,\xef\xbf\xbd affirmed.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to affirm is granted and the judgment is affirmed.
