389 U.S. 580

THRIFTY SHOPPERS SCRIP CO. v. UNITED STATES ET AL.
APPEAL FROM THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE CENTRAL DISTRICT
OF CALIFORNIA. No. 820.
Decided January 15, 1968.*

[Footnote *] Together with No. 849, Twyman, dba Bill\'s Union Station, et al. v. United States et al., also on appeal from the same court.

272 F. Supp. 432, affirmed.

David B. Gold for appellant in No. 820. Howard M. Downs for appellants in No. 849.

Solicitor General Griswold, Assistant Attorney General Turner and Daniel M. Friedman for the United States in both cases. William W. Alsup, Allyn O. Kreps, Arnold M. Lerman, William E. Mussman and Gordon Johnson for appellees Blue Chip Stamp Co. et al. in both cases.

PER CURIAM.

The motions to affirm are granted and the judgment is affirmed.
