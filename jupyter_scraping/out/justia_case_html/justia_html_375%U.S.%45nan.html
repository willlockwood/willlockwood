HESS ET AL. v. KRIZ ET AL.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF OKLAHOMA.
No. 318, Misc.
Decided October 21, 1963.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Reported below: 379 P.2d 851.

Appellants pro se.

Tom W. Garrett for appellees.
