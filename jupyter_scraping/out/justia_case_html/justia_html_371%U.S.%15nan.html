U.S. Supreme Court

BARTON v. DISTRICT COURT, 371 U.S. 15 (1962)

371 U.S. 15

BARTON v. DISTRICT COURT OF IOWA IN AND FOR UNION COUNTY.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF IOWA.
No. 31, Misc.
Decided October 8, 1962.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Appellant pro se.

Evan Hultman, Attorney General of Iowa, for appellee.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.

MR. JUSTICE GOLDBERG took no part in the consideration or decision of this case.
