U.S. Supreme Court

OCEAN CITY v. FEDERAL RESERVE BANK OF PHILADELPHIA, 302 U.S. 752 (1937)

302 U.S. 752

OCEAN CITY, petitioner,
v.
FEDERAL RESERVE BANK OF PHILADELPHIA.*
No. 525.

Supreme Court of the United States

December 6, 1937

Mr. Geo. A. Bourgeois, of Atlantic City, N. J., for petitioner.

For opinion below, see 91 F.2d 635.

Petition for writ of certiorari to the United States Circuit Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit denied, without prejudice to the right to apply for a reinstatement of the petition at any time before the end of this term in case a new trial shall not be awarded.

* Rehearing denied 302 U.S. 781, 58 S.Ct. 475, 82 L.Ed. --.[ Ocean City v. Federal Reserve Bank of Philadelphia 302 U.S. 752 (1937) ]
