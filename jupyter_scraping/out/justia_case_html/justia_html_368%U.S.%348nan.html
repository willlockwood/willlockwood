MOREHOUSE ET AL. v. UNITED STATES ET AL.

No. 500.
Decided December 18, 1961.

194 F. Supp. 940, affirmed.

Clarence D. Todd, G. Duane Vieth and Robert N. Burchmore for appellants.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.
