U.S. Supreme Court

SCHON v. SCHON, 358 U.S. 41 (1958)

358 U.S. 41

SCHON v. SCHON, EXECUTOR.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF FLORIDA.
No. 33, Misc.
Decided October 13, 1958.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Reported below: 99 So.2d 302.

Appellant pro se.

Sanford M. Swerdlin for appellee.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to add the State of Florida as a party appellee is denied. The appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for a writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
