I. L. F. Y. CO. v. RENT COMM\'N, 369 U.S. 795 (1962)

369 U.S. 795

I. L. F. Y. CO. v. TEMPORARY STATE HOUSING RENT COMMISSION ET AL.
APPEAL FROM THE COURT OF APPEALS OF NEW YORK.
No. 569.
Decided May 21, 1962.

Appeal dismissed for want of a substantial federal question.

Reported below: 10 N. Y. 2d 263, 176 N. E. 2d 822.

Robert S. Fougner for appellant.

Harold Zucker, Robert E. Herman and Edward V. Alfieri for the State Rent Commission, appellee.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed for want of a substantial federal question.

MR. JUSTICE FRANKFURTER took no part in the consideration or decision of this case.
