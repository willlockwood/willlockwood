CHAFFIN v. CALIFORNIA, 358 U.S. 642 (1959)

358 U.S. 642

CHAFFIN ET AL. v. CALIFORNIA.
APPEAL FROM THE APPELLATE DEPARTMENT OF THE SUPERIOR COURT OF CALIFORNIA,
COUNTY OF SAN DIEGO. No. 549.
Decided February 24, 1959.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Morris Lavine for appellants.

Aaron W. Reese for appellee.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
