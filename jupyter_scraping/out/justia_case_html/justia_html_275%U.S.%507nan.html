U.S. Supreme Court

CHESAPEAKE & O R. CO. v. LEITCH, 275 U.S. 507 (1927)

275 U.S. 507
No. 98.

CHESAPEAKE & OHIO RAILWAY COMPANY, petitioner,
v.
K. S. LEITCH. *

Supreme Court of the United States

December 12, 1927

Mr. Douglas W. Brown, of Huntington, W. Va., for petitioner.

Mr. Jonn H. Holt, of Huntington, W. Va., for respondent.

The judgment of the Supreme Court of Appeals of the State of West Virginia in this case is affirmed by an equally divided Court.

Footnotes

[Footnote *] Rehearing granted 48 S. Ct. 213, 72 L. Ed. -, and judgment reversed 276 U.S. 429, 48 S. Ct. 336, 72 L. Ed. -.[ Chesapeake & O R Co v. Leitch 275 U.S. 507 (1927) ]
