TURNER ET AL. v. CLAY ET AL.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF SOUTH CAROLINA.
No. 982.
Decided February 24, 1970

253 S. C. 209, 169 S. E. 2d 617, appeal dismissed.

Harry M. Lightsey, Jr., for appellants.
