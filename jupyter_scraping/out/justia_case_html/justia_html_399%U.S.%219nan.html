GENERAL LOCATION\n

\n

The state boundary line involved herein is located between Crittenden County, Arkansas, and Shelby County, Tennessee, in an area formerly known as Cow Island Bend, and more recently called Scanlan Chute, Frog Chute, Ike Chute or Lake, and 96 Chute, and is generally within a rectangle between latitudes 35\xef\xbf\xbd 00\' and 35\xef\xbf\xbd 03\', and longitudes 90\xef\xbf\xbd 15\' and 90\xef\xbf\xbd 19\', and is more particularly described as follows:
