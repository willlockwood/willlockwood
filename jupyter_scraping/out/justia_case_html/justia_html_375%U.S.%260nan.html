375 U.S. 260

McALLISTER v. LOUISIANA.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF LOUISIANA.
No. 721, Misc.
Decided December 16, 1963.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Reported below: 244 La. 42, 150 So.2d 557.

Ernest A. Carrere, Jr. for appellant.
