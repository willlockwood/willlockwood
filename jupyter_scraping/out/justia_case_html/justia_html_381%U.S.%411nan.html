SMITH v. WARDEN, CONNECTICUT STATE PRISON.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPERIOR COURT OF CONNECTICUT, HARTFORD COUNTY.
No. 934, Misc.
Decided June 1, 1965.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

PER CURIAM.
