U.S. Supreme Court

WATKINS v. CITY OF WILSON, 370 U.S. 46 (1962)

370 U.S. 46

WATKINS v. CITY OF WILSON ET AL.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF NORTH CAROLINA.
No. 789.
Decided May 28, 1962.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Reported below: 255 N.C. 510, 121 S. E. 2d 861.

Romallus O. Murphy and Samuel S. Mitchell for appellant.

John F. Doyle for appellees.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.

MR. JUSTICE FRANKFURTER took no part in the consideration or decision of this case.
