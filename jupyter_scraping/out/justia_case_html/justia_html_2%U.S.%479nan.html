U.S. Supreme Court

APPOINTMENT OF PATERSON, 2 U.S. 479 (1793)

2 U.S. 479 (Dall.)

Appointment of PATERSON

August Term, 1793

The Court being met, a commission appointing William Paterson, one of the Justices, bearing date the 4th of March, 1793, was read; and he was qualified according to law.*

Footnotes

[Footnote *] Judge Paterson\'s appointment was in the room of Mr. Justice Johnson, who had resigned.

The Malignant Fever, which during this year, raged in the City of Philadelphia, dispersed the great body of its inhabitants, and proved fatal to thousands, interrupted, likewise, the business of the Courts; and I cannot trace, that any important cause was agitated in the present Term.[ Appointment of Paterson

Footnote 2 U.S. 479 (1793) ]
