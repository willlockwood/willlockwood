OHIO EX REL. IAUS v. CARLTON ET AL.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF OHIO.
No. 726.
Decided April 20, 1959.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Reported below: 168 Ohio St. 279, 154 N. E. 2d 150.

Irwin Greene and Walter L. Greene for appellant.
