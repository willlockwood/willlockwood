U.S. Supreme Court

ARCO AUTO CARRIERS, INC. v. ARKANSAS, 365 U.S. 770 (1961)

365 U.S. 770

ARCO AUTO CARRIERS, INC., ET AL. v. ARKANSAS EX REL. BENNETT, ATTORNEY
GENERAL.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF ARKANSAS. No. 785.
Decided April 17, 1961.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Reported below: 232 Ark. 779, 341 S. W. 2d 15.

George S. Dixon and Louis Tarlowski for appellants.

J. Frank Holt, Attorney General of Arkansas, and Bruce T. Bullion, Special Assistant Attorney General, for appellee.

PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
